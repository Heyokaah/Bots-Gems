📚 Grammar Recap: Defining vs. Non-Defining

Review these quick slides summarized from our class PowerPoint before you start testing your knowledge.

Defining

Essential Information

  • ✔ Tells us exactly which person or thing we are talking about.
  • ✔ If removed, the sentence will not make sense or the main idea completely changes.
  • ❌ We do NOT use commas.
  • ✔ Can use relative pronouns: who, which, whose, and that.
Example: "The boss likes employees who are organized."
Non-Defining

Extra Information

  • ✔ Gives more/extra details about a person or thing.
  • ✔ If removed, the sentence still makes perfect sense.
  • ✔ We MUST use commas to separate the clause.
  • ❌ Never use that! Only use: who, which, whose.
Example: "The boss likes Jack, who is an organized employee, because he works well."
