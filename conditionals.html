Revision Guide

Type 0 (General Truths) Always True

If: Simple Present

Main: Simple Present

"If you heat ice, it melts."

Type 1 (Real Possible) Likely Future

If: Simple Present

Main: Will + Bare Infinitive

"If it rains tomorrow, we will stay home."

Type 2 (Imaginary Present) Unreal Now

If: Simple Past (were)

Main: Would + Bare Infinitive

"If I were you, I would study harder."

Type 3 (Imaginary Past) Regret / Past unreal

If: Past Perfect (had + p.p.)

Main: Would have + Past Participle

"If you had studied, you would have passed."

Advanced S3 Notes

Unless = If ... not
"Unless you run, you'll be late." = "If you don't run..."

Modals: replace 'will' with might (possibility), can (ability/permission), or should (advice).
